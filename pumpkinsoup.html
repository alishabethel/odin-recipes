Pumpkin Soup

This fall favorite is sure to keep you warm on even the chilliest night. Happy cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 mediun onion, finely chopped
  • 1 medium pie pumpkin, peeled and cut into 1" chunks
  • 2 large carrots, peeled and cut into 1" chunks
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 15oz canned pumpkin
  • 12oz evaporated milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Optional: Sour cream and toasted pumpkin seeds

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in the 6-qt. (5.7-L) Enameled Cast Iron Dutch Oven over medium heat for 3–5 minutes. Add the onion. Cook and stir for 3–4 minutes, or until softened.
  2. Add the pumkpkin, carrots, garlic, and thyme. Cook and stir for 1 minute, or until fragrant.
  3. Add the stock. Cover and bring to a boil over high heat. Cook for 20 minutes, or until the squash is very soft, stirring occasionally. Remove from the heat.
  4. Use an immersion or regular blender to blend the cooked ingredients on medium, gradually increasing to high speed, until smooth, about 2–3 minutes.
  5. Add the canned pumpkin, evaporated milk, salt, and pepper, and blend on high, about 1 minute.
  6. Bring the soup to a simmer over medium-high heat. Remove from the heat.
  7. Serve with sour cream and pumpkin seeds, if you’d like.
